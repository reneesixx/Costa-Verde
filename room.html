Our Accomodations


Hotel Costa Verde offers accommodations for a variety of options for vacation living.We have 70 rooms built on jungle ridges and bluffs, positioned to capture the best ocean views and sea breeze. Our rooms are equipped with air-conditioning, cable TV, Wi-Fi, safe deposit boxes, hair dryers, refrigerators, coffee makers, kitchenettes with microwave, our wood shop’s balinese style furniture and spacious private balconies. We have 3 pools on the grounds so one is always close by regardless of where your room is located.


Costa Verde Standard

Costa Verde II is designed for couples and families with children 17 years or older, seeking a romantic getaway with a balmy, tropical ambiance. Each newly renovated apartment features 750 square feet of space with expansive windows, new digital air conditioning, a spacious balcony with a panoramic ocean view, two queen-size beds, kitchenette with breakfast bar and microwave, flat screen TV and living room set of couch and two arm chairs. Guests enjoy an adult-only pool with the best ocean, beach & National Park views in Manuel Antonio.

727 Fuselage Home

The airplane, nestled beside the rain forest and beside our Costa Verde II building, is perched on a custom-built platform 15 meters (50 feet) up from the ground. At this height, its guests will be able to enjoy scenic ocean and jungle views from both the factory airplane windows and the hand crafted deck that has been built atop its wings. The inside of the plane has been decorated using local teak wood and Costa Verde‘s renowned innovation. This has resulted in a beautiful wood paneled fuselage with three queen size beds, two bathrooms, TV, AC, kitchenette and safety deposit.
