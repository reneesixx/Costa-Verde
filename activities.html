Our Activities


There is never a dull moment in Manuel Antonio! Let our hotel staff plan an adventure-filled vacation for you. We can customize your itinerary to make sure the whole family is happy – relaxing spa treatments, high-energy outdoor fun or educational tours.


Damas Estuary Launch

Enjoy the coastline tour on a large shaded boat. Fun for every age. Snorkel in Biesanz Bay and ride the banana boat! Check out the wonders of the ocean like sea turtles, bobbies and frigates as well as dolphins. Morning The local mangrove swamp is a great place to see whitefaced monkeys, silky anteaters and huge crocodiles. Visit this unique ecosystem in the comfort of a shaded boat and an entertaining guide.

Includes: lunch

Time: approximately 4 hours

Price: $65 per person


Yoga & Pilates

Practicing Yoga with Spa Holis at Costa Verde in Manuel Antonio, is an experience combining the sun to heat and soften our tight muscles; the ocean’s rhythm to help us remember a fluid, soothing breath and the sounds of nature and it’s tropical creatures to invite our playful heart to dive into it’s full desire.

Yoga is held Monday through Friday at 8am, Saturday at 10am

Pilates is held Monday, Wednesday and Friday at 7:30am



Horseback Riding

Explore isolated jungles, river and waterfalls by horseback on full-day or half-day tours of the nearby mountains. Includes: experienced guide, breakfast and typical lunch, transportation, and healthy strong horses.

Time: approximately 7 hours for full-day tour

Prices: $65 full-day; $50 per person ½ day.


Sea Kayaking

Kayak the protected mangrove waterways of the Isla Damas Estuary with a guide to show you all the wildlife... or challenge yourself to a more rugged workout on the coastline between Quepos and Manuel Antonio, with some waves and surf.

Time: approximately 6 hours

Price: $69 per person. Includes lunch



Sunset Sailing

Your adventure aboard this comfortable, single hull yacht begins as you sail along the scenic coast past the beautiful beaches and islands of Manuel Antonio National Park. As the boat glides quietly along you may see dolphins, which frequently swim in the boat's wake, and even the occasional whale or giant sea turtle.

Time: 3 - 4 hours

Price: $75 per person. Includes food, beverages & wine



Surf Lessons

Try some surf or boogie board lessons at Manuel Antonio beach, a short walk from Costa verde.

Time: approximately 4 hours

Price: $75 per person. Includes surfboard



Canopy Zip Line Tours

Gliding from platform to platform, secured by your harness, enjoy flying through our local jungle canopies. In the Manuel Antonio area we have five very different zip line tours to choose from

Price: about $70 per person, includes breakfast, lunch, snacks and all transportation.



Waterfall Rappelling

One fall is 150 feet and another is 175 feet high. Enjoy a typical Costa Rican lunch overlooking the swinging bridge canyon waterfall. The canyoning tour is for anyone in moderate physical condition. Kid friendly, no experience necessary. Guides are safety conscience and professionally trained to help curb your fears.

Time: 3 - 4 hours

Price: $75 per person. Includes food, beverages & wine



Jet Ski Safari

Action-packed wave runner jet ski adventure with coastline tour and/or estuary tour.

Time: approximately 2 hours

Price: $95 for single-mounted jet-ski; $135 for double



National Park Guided Tour

Explore Manuel Antonio National Park with an experienced guide in search of sloths, monkeys and exotic birds. The guide will explain the history of the park and its flora and fauna.

Time: approximately 3 hours

Price: $43 per person, includes park entrance fee
